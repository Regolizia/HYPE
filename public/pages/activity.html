Writing poetry

Presentation

"Writing is not easy" they say.
It may be not easy but it's truly freeing. For this reason Youth Speaks Hawai'i offers several events every month where people can start this journey of expressing their thoughts in written form. No boring lessons. This activity focuses on how to form well written phrases to convey meaning.

If you are interested in how this activity is structured and its requirements, visit the Practical info section.
