When

Where
Kawaiahaʻo Plaza, Honolulu

Overview

Welcome back everybody!

After this long winter we are back the first tutoring session on how to compose poetry! During this event not only we'll explain the basics on how to start but moreover we'll talk about how to speak to an audience and defeat anxiety onstage. We are aware of the fact that both newbies and veterans will attend and we are sure to keep everyone entertained and informed.

If you need more information on this event, feel free to contact the main organizer.

Additional information about the location or how to reach are in the Practical info section.
